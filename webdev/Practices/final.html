Park City Ski Resort

Why Park City?

Park City is the largest ski resort in the entire country, spanning 7,300 arcs. There are two sections to Park City: Park City Base Area and Canyons Village.

Park City Base Area has 40 different ski lifts, terrain parks, and over 300 runs! Some of the most popular runs include:

  • Drift (Green-Easy)
  • Claim Jumper (Green-Easy)
  • Georgeanna (Blue-Intermediate)
  • Sunrise (Blue-Intermediate)
  • Hidden Splendor (Blue-Intermediate)
  • Gotcha Cut-Off (Blue-Intermediate)
  • Molly's (Double Black Diamond-Technical)
  • P-Zone (Double Black Diamond-Technical)
  • Constellation (Double Black Diamond-Technical)
  • Portuguese Gap (Double Black Diamond-Technical)
