The Riordanverse

The Riordanverse (also called the Percy Jackson universe) is the universe in which all of the books written by Rick Riordan are held in.

Series included in the Riordanverse:

  1. Percy Jackson and the Olympians
  2. Percy Jackson Heros of Olympus
  3. Percy Jackson College Quests
  4. The Kain Chronicles
  5. Magnus Chase and the Gods of Asgard
  6. The Trials of Apollo
