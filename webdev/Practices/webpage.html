Nemphkar

Nemphkar is a fantasy story in which the main character, Kohnai, leaves nearly half a decade of hiding in order to help her best friend reuinte with his astranged mother and two siblings after the confirmation of his father's death. Although, things are as simple as they might seem. Kohnai wants to stay safe and unknown. Four years after running away from Seigaf and the Nemphkarian Camps, she still looks over her shoulder to make sure nobody is following. All she wants is to be left to a life of anonymity were she can live her life they way it could've been.

Mendekkian Empire

The Mendekkian Empire is onw of the many countries in Penmar, the world in which this story takes place. The Mendekkian Empire, Mendekka, was founded around a thousand years ago by Mendekk Kar-Dreni Tor, the world first demigod.
Tenella Bridge, located in southeast Mendekka is a long series of bridges through the dense Arizidien Mountain range that connects the southern coastal regions to rest of the Mendekkian Empire. This is also one of the large trade cities in the world.
One of the most well known bridges would be Hyrenna's Bridge. This bridge was built in 207 DE by the Empress Regent of the time. It has three layers refered to as: the first bridge, second bridge, and third bridge.
